Documentatiopn & Process

2 Wheeler And 4 Wheeler License
  • Step 1: Learning licence
  • Step 2: Permanent driving licence
  • Common age proofs- 10th Mark sheet, Passport, TC, LIC Bond, Birth certificate, Old driving license, PAN Card (any one compulsory).
  • Local address proof- Election Card, Aadhar card, Ration Card, LIC Bond, Passport, BSNL Bill, Rental agreement and with current bill (any one compulsory).
  • Permanent Address proof (Other State)- Any Government ID proof or sale deed.
  • Note: 5 passport size photographs would also be needed along with the above documents. And if the applicant's age is 40 years and above, a Medical certificate have to be produced.
Duplicate Driving Licence.
  • Proof for having lost driving licence (Acknowledgement/FIR copy for having lodged complaint with Police).
  • Affidavit sworn by the applicant before an executive magistrate for having lost the driving licence.
  • In case of same city, photo copy of lost licence.
  • 5-6 recent passport sized photographs..
Duplicate Driving Licence.
  • Original existing driving licence 3 recent passport size photographs..
  • If applicant's age is 40 years and above, Medical certificate have to be produced.
Change of Address.
  • Address proof: Your Voter card Aadhaar card or Passport mentioning your permanent address would suffice, or your permanent address is same as local address, then only permanent address alone will suffice.
  • Self-swearing Affidavit by an applicant before an executive Magistrate or a First Class Judicial Magistrate or a Notary Public as evidence of age and address in case of submitting local address proof as rental agreement or HR letter.
International Driving Permitt.
  • 3-5 recent passport size photograph.
  • Original Valid Driving Licence + Passport + Visa + Air Ticket. .
  • The applicant should be citizen of India.
  • In case of driving licence belonging to other regions/state, the applicant should apply and obtain change of address in the driving licence. In case of licence issued by other regions within Karnataka state the time required for change of address is three days, and in case of driving licence issued by other states the time required is 21 days. Therefore the applicant keeping the time required in mind should plan well in advance while making an application for IDP.
  • Note:The address in the Driving licence should fall under the jurisdiction of the Regional Transport office to where the application for IDP is being made. Applicant shall appear for re-test if the driving licence issued in within 3 months. Validity of the IDP will be for one year or till the validity of the driving licence whichever occurs first.
